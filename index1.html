Crime in Bangalore

The map

Bangalore is split into several divisions based on the locations of the Deputy Commissioner of Police offices. Crimes and complaints are handles areawise by these offices.

Murders

This map shows the top three areas in Bangalore with the highest number of murders. The highest is 47 in West Bangalore followed by 36 in North Bangalore and 29 in South Bangalore.

Robbery

These are the top three localities which had the most number of robberies in 2018.

Attempted Murders

The number of attempted murders per year is much higher, but follows the same area wise pattern.

Gold Chain Snatching

Chain snatching is a common crime in Bangalore. Thieves on scooters and bikes usually ride past pedetrians and snatch their jewelery. Unsurprisingly the same 3 areas in Bangalore have the highest amount of cases, it seems as though most crime in Bangalore is found in this area.

Complaints Per Day

This shows the areas that file the highest number of police complaints each day, starting with Jayanagar at 6 complaints every single day. Followed by Koramangala and HSR layout.

Violence against Women

3,157 crimes against women transpired in Bangalore in 2015. That means on average, 10 women in the city were molested, sexually assaulted/harassed or subjected to violence each day.
